How This Calculator Works

This calculator determines your position size based on your account size and risk tolerance.

It calculates the stop loss price based on a 5% drop from the entry price, and the take profit price at twice that distance (10% gain).

The position size is determined by dividing your risk amount by the difference between entry and stop loss prices.
